System Requirements

Mobile App Requirements
Android:
  • Android 8.0 (Oreo) or later
  • 2GB RAM or more
  • 100MB free storage space
  • Camera with autofocus
  • Internet connection for analysis
iOS:
  • iOS 13.0 or later
  • iPhone 7 or newer
  • iPad 5th generation or newer
  • 100MB free storage space
  • Internet connection for analysis
Web App Requirements
Browser Compatibility:
  • Chrome 70 or later
  • Firefox 65 or later
  • Safari 12 or later
  • Edge 79 or later
Additional Requirements:
  • JavaScript enabled
  • WebGL support for 3D models
  • Camera access for image capture
  • Internet connection
  • Minimum 4GB RAM recommended

Installation Instructions

For Mobile Devices:

1
Find our app

Open Google Play Store (Android) or App Store (iOS) and search for "Archaeological Artifact Identifier"

2
Download and install

Tap the "Install" or "Get" button to download and install the app on your device

3
Open the app

Once installed, tap "Open" or find the app icon on your home screen and tap to launch

4
Create an account

Follow the on-screen instructions to create your account and start using the app

For Web Version:

1
Open your browser

Launch Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or any other supported browser

2
Navigate to our website

Go to www.artifactidentifier.com in your browser's address bar

3
Bookmark the page

For easy access, bookmark the website in your browser

4
Optional: Add to home screen

On mobile browsers, use the "Add to Home Screen" option for app-like access

App Features

Image Capture

Use your device's camera to capture artifact images directly within the app

AI Analysis

Advanced artificial intelligence identifies and classifies artifacts with high accuracy

Carbon Dating

Calculate approximate age of organic materials using our carbon-14 dating tool

Regional Database

Access information about archaeological finds in your region

Historical Timeline

Explore different historical eras and their characteristic artifacts

PDF Reports

Generate comprehensive PDF reports of your artifact analyses

Cloud Sync

Sync your data across multiple devices with secure cloud storage

Sharing

Easily share your findings with colleagues or on social media
